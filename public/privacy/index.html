ReturnIt 🛍

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

The short version: ReturnIt stores all your data on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal information. There are no accounts, no servers, and no tracking.

1. What We Collect

ReturnIt stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Return and rebate items you add (store name, item description, dates, amounts)
  • Receipt photos you optionally attach
  • Your total savings amount
  • Notification preferences

This data never leaves your device. It is stored in your browser's local storage and is not transmitted to any server.

2. What We Do Not Collect

  • No name, email address, or account information
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No crash reports sent off-device
  • No cookies or third-party trackers

3. Camera & Photo Library

ReturnIt may request access to your camera and photo library solely to let you attach receipt photos to return or rebate items. Photos are stored locally on your device only. We never upload photos to any server.

4. Notifications

ReturnIt may request permission to send local notifications to remind you of upcoming return and rebate deadlines. These notifications are generated entirely on your device — no data is sent to any server to deliver them. You can disable notifications at any time in your iPhone Settings.

5. Data Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all app data at any time by:

  • Deleting individual items within the app
  • Uninstalling the app, which removes all locally stored data

6. Children's Privacy

ReturnIt is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
